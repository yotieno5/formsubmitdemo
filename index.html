Company Profile

Ndoju Infrastructural Company Limited (NICoL), is a Private Limited company incorporated under the companies act, 2015 in Kenya. NICoL is duly licensed by NATIONAL CONSTRUCTION AUTHORITY (NCA) to carry out works and projects as a building and construction company.

We carry out consultancy in General Engineering in the construction industry ranging from Civil, Architectural, Mechanical, Electrical Works, site management, building material supplies, technical work guidance and general on-site recommendations.

We undertake energy production and development projects on consultancy basis, including recommendations of suitable fuel to be applied or used in different powering systems and conditions, (Solar, Coal, Petroleum). We do consultancy jobs either as full contractors or sub-contractors on project management, building, construction, Fuel stations developments, structural and general fabrication for individuals, Private cooperations, NGO's, Public Private Partnerships projects, major Construction companies and Government sponsored roads and Building projects.

We are situated in Nairobi, Kahawa, Garissa Road, 4 th South Avenue, Plot No. 3411 Nairobi County, and we believe in delivering, proven and high-quality structures and cutting-edge consultancy services in the Energy, construction and Infrastructural development sectors.
